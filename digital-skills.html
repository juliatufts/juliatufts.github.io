Notes

We learned how to make our websites more responsive using fluid components and media queries. Some things to note:

  • Always remember to include the viewport meta tag in the head section your HTML
  • Order matters! If you have several media queries that overlap in width, the last one will override any previous ones.

We also learned how to display and position background images. Here are some good examples of what you can do with background images:

  • The "Hero" image
  • Simple Parallax, How-to and Demo
  • Use a textured background

You can indeed use SVGs as background images! CSS-Tricks has a good example on this.
